08. Asynchronous, Concurrent, or Parallel?

Asynchronous, Concurrent, or Parallel?

Lets get some terms straight. These terms are similar enough in meaning that they often get mixed up, misused, and abused, but this course wouldn't be complete without making sure that you are equipped to use each term correctly and understand the differences between them.

ND032 JSND L1 A07 Asynchronous Concurrent Or Parallel

Recap

We covered a lot of new terms in this lesson, lets go over them again.

Asynchronous

Refers to the management of a single thread that can move past tasks that are waiting to complete the rest of the program. Synonymous with non-blocking.

Parallel

Running two processes on two separate threads simultaneously

Concurrent

Achieving the appearance of simultaneous computation by switching between tasks on an active thread
